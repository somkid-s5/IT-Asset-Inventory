Dell PowerEdge R760 Technical Specifications Manual
===================================================

Processor: Dual 4th Generation Intel Xeon Scalable Processors (Up to 32 Cores per CPU)
Memory: 32x DDR5 DIMM slots, supports RDIMM up to 8TB
Storage Controllers: PERC 11 & PERC 12 controllers (H965i, H755, H355)
Drive Bays: Up to 8x 2.5-inch SAS/SATA/NVMe or 16x 2.5-inch SAS/SATA/NVMe
Power Supplies: Dual Redundant Hot-Pluggable Titanium PSUs (800W, 1100W, 1400W)
Management: iDRAC9 Enterprise with Lifecycle Controller
Dimensions: 2U rack form factor
